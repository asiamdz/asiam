About ASIAM

The Global Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ASIAM) builds cooperation between mathematics and the worlds of science and technology.

Founded to foster strong interactions between mathematics and other scientific and technological communities, ASIAM accomplishes this through its publications, conferences, and diverse membership programs. We support researchers, educators, and students worldwide.

Activity Groups (SIAGs)

Our members network and collaborate through specialized Activity Groups, including:

  • Computational Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Data Science (DS)
  • Optimization (OPT)
  • Financial Mathematics and Engineering (FME)
